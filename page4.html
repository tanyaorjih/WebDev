3.2 Describe ways to improve productivity and efficiency

Productivity and efficiency - These are two separate tasks that usually seem to go hand in hand. Productivity is achieved by completing tasks or mile stones in a short period of time and being efficient means finding the best was of doing a particular task in relation to a given constrain

List of ways to improve productivity include;

Write relevant code into modules that can be quickly reused.

Assigning your time to the most relevant tasks.

Investing time in yourself by continuously learning and keeping up-to-date.

Taking breaks as often as possible in order to rewire your brain’s synapsis to reduce the possibility of burn out.

3.4 Describe how you would go about testing digital solutions

Given the nature of the project the two least resource intensive way of testing the website was Alpha Testing and betta testing.

Alpha Testing: This type of testing is when the developer goes through each component of the website making functionality checks, making sure things like the page adjust when the browser frame is resized.

Betta Testing: At its core this is getting someone else to use your product in order in order to see what works and what those not.

I tested the website by conducting the Alpha testing in the Google Chrome and Microsoft Edge (internet explorer) browser and all functionality works
